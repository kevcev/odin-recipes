Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich consists of peanut butter and fruit preserves—jelly—spread on bread.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter (Creamy or Crunchy)
  • Jelly
  • 2 pieces of bread

Steps

  1. Spread jelly evenly on one piece of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly on the other piece of bread
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich
