Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled cheese is a hot sandwich made with buttered and toasted bread and originally filled with American cheese, but is now commonly made with one or more different cheeses.

Ingredients

  • American Cheese
  • Butter
  • 2 pieces of bread

Steps

  1. Let butter melt in pan
  2. Make a sandwich with the cheese in the middle of both slices
  3. Cook the sandwich on the pan until golden toasted
