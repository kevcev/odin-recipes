French Onion Soup

Description

French onion soup is a soup usually based on meat stock and onions, and often served gratin√©ed with croutons or a larger piece of bread covered with cheese floating on top.

Ingredients

  • 6 large red or yellow onions
  • 4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 8 cups of beef stock

Steps

  1. Caramelize the onions
  2. Deglaze the pot with vermouth or wine
  3. Add the stock, bay leaves, and thyme
  4. Toast the French bread slices
  5. Serve
